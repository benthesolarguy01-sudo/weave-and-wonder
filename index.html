Our Story

Two sisters, one big dream.

Hi! We're Bethany (12) and Ella (10), two sisters from Coomba Park on the beautiful Mid North Coast of New South Wales, Australia.

While travelling in Guatemala, we fell in love with the art of making friendship bracelets. The colours, the patterns, the joy of creating something with your own hands — it was magic. We knew we had to share it with kids back home.

That's how Weave & Wonder was born. We design and make every kit ourselves, using beautiful Tasmanian Oak for our looms and premium wax thread in vibrant colours. We want to give kids a fun, creative way to spend time away from screens — making something real that they can share with the people they love.

🇯🇵 Our Big Goal: Every kit you buy helps us attend a leadership program in Japan, where we'll learn about business, creativity, and global citizenship. Thank you for being part of our journey!

What's in the Kit?

Each kit comes with a hand-stamped Tasmanian Oak loom and everything you need to get started.

✓

Tasmanian Oak Base Board — 30cm x 6.5cm, sanded smooth. Heat-stamped with the Weave & Wonder logo.

✓

2 x Wooden Dowels — 13cm tall. Slot into pre-drilled holes for quick assembly (no glue needed!).

✓

Wax Thread — 6 colours of premium wax thread (4.6m per colour). Extended Kit includes 12 colours! Enough to make multiple bracelets.

✓

Illustrated Instructions — Step-by-step guide to making your first bracelet, from setup to finish.

✓

Ships Flat-Packed — Assembles in seconds. Fits in an Australia Post small satchel.
